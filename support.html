Troubleshooting

  • Subscriptions: iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions (manage, cancel, or restore).
  • Restore purchases: if the app includes a Restore button, try it after reinstalling.
  • App not saving changes: force-close the app and reopen; ensure you’re on the latest version.
  • Crashes or freezing: restart your phone; update iOS; reinstall the app.

Account & Data

Weed It Out is designed to minimize data collection and provide habit tracking only. If you need help with data access or deletion requests (if applicable), email support.
